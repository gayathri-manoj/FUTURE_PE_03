## Conversation 1: Biology Topic

User: Explain photosynthesis  
Bot: Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants make their own food using sunlight. It takes place mainly in the leaves, where chlorophyll absorbs light energy and helps convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen.

---

## Conversation 2: Physics Topic

User: What is Newton’s first law?  
Bot: Newton’s first law states that an object will remain at rest or continue moving at a constant speed in a straight line unless acted upon by an external force. This law explains why seatbelts are important in moving vehicles.
